# Industry Context & KPI Interpretation

## Retail Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Revenue per Square Foot**: Sales efficiency
- **Inventory Turnover**: How fast inventory sells
- **Average Transaction Value**: Customer spending
- **Units per Transaction**: Basket size
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitors to buyers
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to get new customer
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total customer value

### Leading Indicators
- Website traffic trends
- Cart abandonment rate
- Email open rates
- Social media engagement
- Foot traffic (if physical stores)

### Lagging Indicators
- Monthly/quarterly revenue
- Customer retention rate
- Profit margins
- Market share

### Warning Thresholds
- Inventory turnover < 4x/year: Overstocking
- Conversion rate < 2%: Poor UX or pricing
- CAC > CLV/3: Unsustainable acquisition
- Return rate > 15%: Quality or expectation issues

### Common Root Causes
- **Sales Decline**: Seasonality, competition, marketing, economy
- **Margin Compression**: Pricing pressure, cost increases, mix shift
- **Inventory Issues**: Demand forecasting errors, supplier problems
- **Customer Churn**: Service issues, competition, price sensitivity

## E-commerce Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitor to buyer percentage
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Revenue per transaction
- **Cart Abandonment Rate**: Started but not completed
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Marketing cost per customer
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue per customer
- **Repeat Purchase Rate**: Loyalty indicator
- **Revenue per Visitor**: Overall monetization

### Leading Indicators
- Add-to-cart rate
- Wishlist additions
- Email signup rate
- Search volume on site
- Product view depth

### Lagging Indicators
- Monthly revenue
- Customer count
- Average order value
- Return rate

### Warning Thresholds
- Cart abandonment > 70%: Checkout friction
- Conversion rate < 1%: Traffic quality or UX issues
- CAC > CLV/3: Unsustainable growth
- Return rate > 20%: Product-description mismatch

### Common Root Causes
- **Low Conversion**: Poor UX, slow loading, trust issues, pricing
- **High Cart Abandonment**: Unexpected costs, complex checkout, security concerns
- **Low AOV**: Poor cross-sell, no bundling, weak recommendations
- **High CAC**: Competitive market, broad targeting, weak funnel

---

## SaaS Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Predictable revenue
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Annualized MRR
- **Churn Rate**: Customer loss percentage
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Expansion vs contraction
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Sales + marketing cost
- **CAC Payback Period**: Months to recover CAC
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Unit economics health

### Leading Indicators
- Product usage/engagement
- Support ticket trends
- Feature adoption rates
- NPS scores
- Login frequency

### Lagging Indicators
- MRR growth
- Churn rate
- Customer count
- Revenue growth

### Warning Thresholds
- Monthly churn > 5%: Product-market fit issues
- NRR < 100%: Net customer revenue loss
- CAC payback > 18 months: Cash flow pressure
- LTV:CAC < 3: Unsustainable unit economics

### Common Root Causes
- **High Churn**: Poor onboarding, missing features, competition
- **Low Expansion**: Limited upsell paths, feature plateau
- **Slow Growth**: Weak marketing, long sales cycles, market saturation
- **Margin Pressure**: High support costs, infrastructure costs

---

## Financial Services

### Key Metrics
- **Assets Under Management (AUM)**: Total managed assets
- **Net Interest Margin (NIM)**: Lending profitability
- **Loan Loss Provision**: Expected defaults
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost per new account
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total customer value
- **Cost-to-Income Ratio**: Operational efficiency
- **Return on Equity (ROE)**: Shareholder returns

### Leading Indicators
- Application volume
- Approval rates
- Market sentiment indices
- Economic indicators
- Regulatory changes

### Lagging Indicators
- Quarterly earnings
- Default rates
- Customer growth
- Revenue growth

### Warning Thresholds
- Default rate > 3%: Credit quality issues
- NIM < 2%: Margin compression
- Cost-to-income > 60%: Operational inefficiency
- ROE < 10%: Underperformance

### Common Root Causes
- **Revenue Decline**: Rate compression, volume decline, fee pressure
- **Rising Defaults**: Economic downturn, credit quality deterioration
- **Customer Loss**: Service issues, competition, digital disruption
- **Margin Pressure**: Regulatory costs, technology investment, competition

---

## Healthcare Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Patient Satisfaction (HCAHPS)**: Quality indicator
- **Readmission Rate**: Care quality
- **Average Length of Stay (ALOS)**: Efficiency
- **Cost per Case**: Financial efficiency
- **Bed Occupancy Rate**: Utilization
- **Staff-to-Patient Ratio**: Quality of care
- **Revenue per Patient**: Financial performance

### Leading Indicators
- Appointment volume
- Emergency department visits
- Staff turnover
- Patient wait times
- Insurance approval times

### Lagging Indicators
- Patient outcomes
- Financial performance
- Quality scores
- Readmission rates

### Warning Thresholds
- Readmission rate > 15%: Quality issues
- Bed occupancy > 85%: Capacity strain
- Staff turnover > 20%: Retention problems
- Patient satisfaction < 70%: Care quality issues

### Common Root Causes
- **Quality Issues**: Staffing, training, process gaps
- **Financial Pressure**: Payer mix, utilization, costs
- **Patient Dissatisfaction**: Wait times, communication, access
- **Operational Inefficiency**: Workflow bottlenecks, technology gaps

---

## Manufacturing Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)**: Production efficiency
- **First Pass Yield (FPY)**: Quality rate
- **Cycle Time**: Production speed
- **Inventory Turns**: Inventory efficiency
- **Scrap Rate**: Waste measurement
- **On-Time Delivery**: Customer satisfaction
- **Cost per Unit**: Production cost

### Leading Indicators
- Machine downtime trends
- Defect rates
- Supplier lead times
- Order backlog
- Employee overtime

### Lagging Indicators
- Monthly production volume
- Quality metrics
- Delivery performance
- Cost per unit

### Warning Thresholds
- OEE < 65%: Significant efficiency loss
- FPY < 95%: Quality problems
- Scrap rate > 5%: Process issues
- On-time delivery < 90%: Customer satisfaction risk

### Common Root Causes
- **Quality Issues**: Process variation, equipment problems, training
- **Efficiency Loss**: Downtime, bottlenecks, poor scheduling
- **Delivery Problems**: Supply chain issues, capacity constraints
- **Cost Overruns**: Waste, rework, inefficiency

## Marketing Industry

### Key Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost per new customer
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Advertising efficiency
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitor to customer
- **Brand Awareness**: Market recognition
- **Engagement Rate**: Content effectiveness
- **Lead Quality**: Sales readiness
- **Marketing ROI**: Overall return

### Leading Indicators
- Website traffic trends
- Social media engagement
- Email open rates
- Content downloads
- Search rankings

### Lagging Indicators
- Customer acquisition
- Revenue attribution
- Brand awareness surveys
- Market share

### Warning Thresholds
- CAC > CLV/3: Unsustainable acquisition
- ROAS < 3: Poor advertising efficiency
- Conversion rate < 2%: Funnel problems
- Engagement rate < 1%: Content issues

### Common Root Causes
- **Low ROI**: Poor targeting, weak creative, wrong channels
- **High CAC**: Competitive market, broad targeting, weak funnel
- **Low Conversion**: Poor landing pages, weak offers, trust issues
- **Low Engagement**: Irrelevant content, wrong timing, channel mismatch

## Cross-Industry Patterns

### Universal Warning Signs
1. **Declining customer satisfaction**: Leading indicator of churn
2. **Increasing costs without revenue growth**: Margin erosion
3. **Rising employee turnover**: Operational risk
4. **Market share loss**: Competitive weakness
5. **Cash flow deterioration**: Financial risk

### Common Analysis Approaches
1. **Cohort Analysis**: Track groups over time
2. **Segmentation**: Identify distinct groups
3. **Trend Analysis**: Understand direction
4. **Root Cause Analysis**: Find underlying causes
5. **Benchmarking**: Compare to standards

### Data Requirements by Analysis Type
1. **Descriptive**: Historical data, current state
2. **Diagnostic**: Detailed data, context, alternatives
3. **Predictive**: Historical patterns, external factors
4. **Prescriptive**: Constraints, objectives, options
