# Supply Chain KPIs

## Delivery Metrics

### On-Time Delivery (OTD)
- **Formula**: Orders Delivered on Time / Total Orders × 100%
- **Purpose**: Delivery performance
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

### Perfect Order Rate
- **Formula**: Orders meeting all criteria (on time, complete, damage-free, correct documentation) / Total Orders × 100%
- **Purpose**: Order quality
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

### Fill Rate
- **Formula**: Units Shipped from Stock / Units Ordered × 100%
- **Purpose**: Stock availability
- **Benchmarks**: >98% is excellent

### Order Cycle Time
- **Formula**: Time from order placement to delivery
- **Purpose**: Delivery speed
- **Use**: Customer service

### Lead Time
- **Formula**: Time from order to receipt
- **Purpose**: Supply chain speed
- **Use**: Planning

### On-Time In-Full (OTIF)
- **Formula**: Orders delivered on time and in full / Total orders × 100%
- **Purpose**: Complete delivery performance
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

## Inventory Metrics

### Inventory Turns
- **Formula**: COGS / Average Inventory
- **Purpose**: Inventory efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Days of Inventory (DOI)
- **Formula**: (Average Inventory / COGS) × Days
- **Purpose**: Inventory days
- **Benchmarks**: Lower is better

### Weeks of Supply
- **Formula**: (Average Inventory / COGS) × 7
- **Purpose**: Inventory in weeks
- **Use**: Planning

### Inventory Accuracy
- **Formula**: (Items Counted / Items in System) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Inventory management
- **Benchmarks**: >98% is excellent

### Stockout Rate
- **Formula**: Number of Stockouts / Total SKUs × 100%
- **Purpose**: Stock availability
- **Benchmarks**: <2% is good

### Excess Inventory
- **Formula**: Inventory above safety stock / Total Inventory × 100%
- **Purpose**: Inventory optimization
- **Use**: Cost reduction

### Obsolete Inventory
- **Formula**: Inventory with no movement in X months / Total Inventory × 100%
- **Purpose**: Inventory health
- **Use**: Write-off decisions

## Cost Metrics

### Total Supply Chain Cost
- **Formula**: Procurement + Manufacturing + Logistics + Inventory Carrying Costs
- **Purpose**: Total cost
- **Use**: Cost optimization

### Cost as % of Revenue
- **Formula**: Total Supply Chain Cost / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Cost efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 10-20% for manufacturing

### Freight Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Freight Cost / Units Shipped
- **Purpose**: Shipping efficiency
- **Use**: Carrier negotiation

### Warehousing Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Warehousing Cost / Units Stored
- **Purpose**: Storage efficiency
- **Use**: Facility optimization

### Inventory Carrying Cost
- **Formula**: (Storage + Insurance + Obsolescence + Opportunity Cost) / Average Inventory Value × 100%
- **Purpose**: Inventory cost
- **Benchmarks**: 20-30% annually

### Procurement Cost per Order
- **Formula**: Total Procurement Costs / Number of Orders
- **Purpose**: Purchasing efficiency
- **Use**: Process improvement

## Supplier Metrics

### Supplier On-Time Delivery
- **Formula**: Supplier Deliveries on Time / Total Supplier Deliveries × 100%
- **Purpose**: Supplier performance
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

### Supplier Quality Rate
- **Formula**: Supplier Deliveries without Defects / Total Supplier Deliveries × 100%
- **Purpose**: Supplier quality
- **Benchmarks**: >99% is excellent

### Supplier Lead Time
- **Formula**: Average time from order to delivery from supplier
- **Purpose**: Supplier speed
- **Use**: Planning

### Supplier Defect Rate
- **Formula**: Defective Items from Supplier / Total Items from Supplier × 100%
- **Purpose**: Supplier quality
- **Benchmarks**: <1% is good

### Supplier Cost Competitiveness
- **Formula**: Supplier Price / Market Average Price
- **Purpose**: Cost comparison
- **Use**: Negotiation

### Supplier Concentration Risk
- **Formula**: Spend with Top Supplier / Total Spend
- **Purpose**: Risk assessment
- **Benchmarks**: <30% for single supplier

## Forecast Accuracy Metrics

### Forecast Accuracy
- **Formula**: 1 - |Actual - Forecast| / Actual
- **Purpose**: Forecast quality
- **Benchmarks**: >90% is excellent

### Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE)
- **Formula**: Σ|Actual - Forecast| / Actual / n × 100%
- **Purpose**: Forecast error
- **Use**: Model selection

### Forecast Bias
- **Formula**: Σ(Forecast - Actual) / n
- **Purpose**: Systematic over/under forecasting
- **Target**: Close to zero

### Forecast Value Added
- **Formula**: Improvement over baseline forecast
- **Purpose**: Forecast process value
- **Use**: Process improvement

### Demand Forecast Accuracy by SKU
- **Formula**: Forecast accuracy at SKU level
- **Purpose**: SKU-level accuracy
- **Use**: Inventory optimization

## Logistics Metrics

### Transportation Cost per Mile
- **Formula**: Total Transportation Cost / Total Miles
- **Purpose**: Transportation efficiency
- **Use**: Carrier negotiation

### Vehicle Utilization
- **Formula**: Actual Load / Vehicle Capacity × 100%
- **Purpose**: Transportation efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: >85% is good

### Load Factor
- **Formula**: Ton-Miles / Capacity-Miles
- **Purpose**: Transportation efficiency
- **Use**: Route optimization

### Freight Cost as % of Revenue
- **Formula**: Total Freight Cost / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Freight efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 5-10% for manufacturing

### Delivery Density
- **Formula**: Number of Deliveries / Route Miles
- **Purpose**: Route efficiency
- **Use**: Route optimization

### Cross-Dock Utilization
- **Formula**: Cross-Docked Volume / Total Volume × 100%
- **Purpose**: Cross-dock efficiency
- **Use**: Facility optimization

## Warehouse Metrics

### Order Picking Accuracy
- **Formula**: Orders Picked Correctly / Total Orders Picked × 100%
- **Purpose**: Picking quality
- **Benchmarks**: >99% is excellent

### Order Picking Speed
- **Formula**: Orders Picked / Labor Hours
- **Purpose**: Picking efficiency
- **Use**: Labor planning

### Warehouse Utilization
- **Formula**: Used Space / Total Space × 100%
- **Purpose**: Space efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 80-90% is optimal

### Dock-to-Stock Time
- **Formula**: Time from receiving to available for picking
- **Purpose**: Receiving efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: <24 hours is good

### Put-Away Time
- **Formula**: Time from receiving to storage location
- **Purpose**: Put-away efficiency
- **Use**: Process improvement

### Returns Processing Time
- **Formula**: Time from return receipt to processed
- **Purpose**: Returns efficiency
- **Use**: Process improvement

## Supply Chain Resilience Metrics

### Supply Chain Risk Score
- **Formula**: Weighted average of risk factors
- **Purpose**: Risk assessment
- **Use**: Risk mitigation

### Supplier Redundancy
- **Formula**: Number of qualified suppliers per critical component
- **Purpose**: Supply security
- **Benchmarks**: >2 for critical components

### Safety Stock Coverage
- **Formula**: Safety Stock / Average Demand
- **Purpose**: Buffer adequacy
- **Use**: Inventory planning

### Lead Time Variability
- **Formula**: Standard Deviation of Lead Times
- **Purpose**: Supply reliability
- **Use**: Safety stock calculation

### Supply Chain Agility
- **Formula**: Time to respond to demand changes
- **Purpose**: Responsiveness
- **Use**: Competitive advantage

### Recovery Time
- **Formula**: Time to return to normal after disruption
- **Purpose**: Resilience
- **Use**: Risk planning

## Sustainability Metrics

### Carbon Footprint per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Carbon Emissions / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Environmental impact
- **Use**: Sustainability reporting

### Green Logistics Percentage
- **Formula**: Green Logistics Spend / Total Logistics Spend × 100%
- **Purpose**: Sustainability
- **Use**: Sustainability strategy

### Packaging Efficiency
- **Formula**: Product Volume / Package Volume
- **Purpose**: Packaging optimization
- **Use**: Cost and environmental reduction

### Reverse Logistics Efficiency
- **Formula**: Returns Processed / Total Returns
- **Purpose**: Returns management
- **Use**: Sustainability

### Sustainable Sourcing Percentage
- **Formula**: Sustainable Materials / Total Materials × 100%
- **Purpose**: Sustainable procurement
- **Use**: Sustainability reporting
