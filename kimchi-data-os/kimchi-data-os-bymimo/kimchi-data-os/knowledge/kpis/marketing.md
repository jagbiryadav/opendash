# Marketing KPIs

## Acquisition Metrics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Formula**: Total Marketing Spend / Number of New Customers
- **Purpose**: Efficiency of customer acquisition
- **Benchmarks**: Should be < LTV/3
- **Use**: Budget allocation, channel optimization

### Cost per Lead (CPL)
- **Formula**: Total Marketing Spend / Number of Leads
- **Purpose**: Lead generation efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Varies by industry and channel

### Cost per Click (CPC)
- **Formula**: Ad Spend / Number of Clicks
- **Purpose**: Paid advertising efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: $0.50-$2.00 average, varies by industry

### Cost per Mille (CPM)
- **Formula**: (Ad Spend / Impressions) × 1000
- **Purpose**: Brand advertising efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: $5-$15 average

### Cost per Acquisition (CPA)
- **Formula**: Ad Spend / Number of Conversions
- **Purpose**: Conversion efficiency
- **Use**: Campaign optimization

## Engagement Metrics

### Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- **Formula**: (Clicks / Impressions) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Ad/content effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 1-3% for display, 3-5% for search

### Conversion Rate
- **Formula**: (Conversions / Visitors) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Website/landing page effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 2-5% for B2B, 1-3% for B2C

### Bounce Rate
- **Formula**: (Single-page Sessions / Total Sessions) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Content relevance
- **Benchmarks**: <40% is excellent, 40-60% is average

### Time on Page
- **Formula**: Average time spent on page
- **Purpose**: Content engagement
- **Use**: Content quality assessment

### Pages per Session
- **Formula**: Total Pageviews / Total Sessions
- **Purpose**: Site engagement
- **Use**: UX optimization

## Email Marketing Metrics

### Open Rate
- **Formula**: (Emails Opened / Emails Delivered) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Subject line effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 20-25% average

### Click-to-Open Rate (CTOR)
- **Formula**: (Email Clicks / Email Opens) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Content effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 10-15% average

### Unsubscribe Rate
- **Formula**: (Unsubscribes / Emails Delivered) × 100%
- **Purpose**: List health
- **Benchmarks**: <0.5% per campaign

### Email Conversion Rate
- **Formula**: (Conversions from Email / Emails Delivered) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Email ROI
- **Use**: Campaign optimization

### List Growth Rate
- **Formula**: ((New Subscribers - Unsubscribes) / Total List) × 100%
- **Purpose**: List health and growth
- **Use**: Growth forecasting

## Social Media Metrics

### Follower Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (New Followers / Total Followers) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Audience growth
- **Use**: Brand awareness

### Engagement Rate
- **Formula**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers × 100%
- **Purpose**: Content resonance
- **Benchmarks**: 1-3% average

### Reach
- **Formula**: Number of unique users who saw content
- **Purpose**: Content visibility
- **Use**: Brand awareness

### Impressions
- **Formula**: Number of times content was displayed
- **Purpose**: Content exposure
- **Use**: Brand recall

### Share of Voice
- **Formula**: Brand Mentions / Total Industry Mentions × 100%
- **Purpose**: Competitive visibility
- **Use**: Brand positioning

## SEO Metrics

### Organic Traffic
- **Formula**: Visitors from organic search
- **Purpose**: SEO effectiveness
- **Use**: Content strategy

### Keyword Rankings
- **Formula**: Position in search results for target keywords
- **Purpose**: SEO performance
- **Use**: Content optimization

### Domain Authority
- **Formula**: Score predicting ranking potential (0-100)
- **Purpose**: SEO strength
- **Use**: Competitive analysis

### Backlink Count
- **Formula**: Number of external links to site
- **Purpose**: SEO authority
- **Use**: Link building strategy

### Organic Conversion Rate
- **Formula**: (Conversions from Organic / Organic Visitors) × 100%
- **Purpose**: SEO ROI
- **Use**: Content monetization

## Content Marketing Metrics

### Content Engagement
- **Formula**: Time on page + Scroll depth + Social shares
- **Purpose**: Content quality
- **Use**: Content strategy

### Content Shares
- **Formula**: Number of social shares
- **Purpose**: Content virality
- **Use**: Content optimization

### Content Downloads
- **Formula**: Number of gated content downloads
- **Purpose**: Lead generation
- **Use**: Content ROI

### Content-attributed Revenue
- **Formula**: Revenue from content-influenced conversions
- **Purpose**: Content ROI
- **Use**: Budget allocation

## Brand Metrics

### Brand Awareness
- **Formula**: Aided/Unaided brand recall
- **Purpose**: Brand recognition
- **Use**: Brand health

### Brand Sentiment
- **Formula**: Positive mentions / Total mentions
- **Purpose**: Brand perception
- **Use**: Reputation management

### Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- **Formula**: % Promoters - % Detractors
- **Purpose**: Customer loyalty
- **Benchmarks**: >50 is excellent

### Share of Market
- **Formula**: Company Revenue / Total Market Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Competitive position
- **Use**: Strategy

## Campaign Metrics

### Campaign ROI
- **Formula**: (Revenue from Campaign - Campaign Cost) / Campaign Cost × 100%
- **Purpose**: Campaign profitability
- **Use**: Budget allocation

### Campaign Reach
- **Formula**: Number of unique users exposed to campaign
- **Purpose**: Campaign awareness
- **Use**: Media planning

### Campaign Frequency
- **Formula**: Average number of times user exposed to campaign
- **Purpose**: Campaign saturation
- **Use**: Media planning

### Campaign Attribution
- **Formula**: Revenue attributed to campaign using attribution model
- **Purpose**: Campaign effectiveness
- **Use**: Budget allocation

## Customer Retention Metrics

### Customer Retention Rate
- **Formula**: (Customers at End - New Customers) / Customers at Start × 100%
- **Purpose**: Customer loyalty
- **Use**: Valuation, growth prediction

### Customer Churn Rate
- **Formula**: Customers Lost / Customers at Start × 100%
- **Purpose**: Customer attrition
- **Benchmarks**: <5% monthly for SaaS

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Formula**: Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
- **Purpose**: Long-term customer value
- **Use**: Budget allocation, segmentation

### Repeat Purchase Rate
- **Formula**: Customers with 2+ Purchases / Total Customers × 100%
- **Purpose**: Customer loyalty
- **Use**: Retention strategy

### Purchase Frequency
- **Formula**: Total Orders / Total Customers
- **Purpose**: Customer engagement
- **Use**: Retention strategy

## Marketing Efficiency Metrics

### Marketing ROI
- **Formula**: (Revenue from Marketing - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost × 100%
- **Purpose**: Marketing profitability
- **Use**: Budget allocation

### Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- **Formula**: Revenue from Ads / Ad Spend
- **Purpose**: Ad profitability
- **Benchmarks**: >3.0 is healthy

### Marketing Efficiency Ratio
- **Formula**: Marketing Spend / Revenue
- **Purpose**: Marketing efficiency
- **Use**: Budget planning

### Customer Acquisition Cost Payback
- **Formula**: CAC / Average Monthly Revenue per Customer
- **Purpose**: CAC recovery time
- **Benchmarks**: <12 months is healthy

## Digital Analytics Metrics

### Website Traffic
- **Formula**: Number of visitors
- **Purpose**: Online presence
- **Use**: Growth tracking

### Traffic Sources
- **Formula**: Visitors by source (organic, paid, social, direct, referral)
- **Purpose**: Channel effectiveness
- **Use**: Channel strategy

### Mobile vs Desktop
- **Formula**: Visitors by device type
- **Purpose**: Device usage
- **Use**: UX optimization

### Site Speed
- **Formula**: Page load time
- **Purpose**: User experience
- **Benchmarks**: <3 seconds

### Conversion Funnel
- **Formula**: Visitors → Leads → Customers at each stage
- **Purpose**: Funnel optimization
- **Use**: Conversion rate optimization
