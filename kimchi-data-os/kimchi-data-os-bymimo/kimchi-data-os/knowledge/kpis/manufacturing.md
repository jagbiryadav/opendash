# Manufacturing KPIs

## Production Metrics

### Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
- **Formula**: Availability × Performance × Quality
- **Purpose**: Manufacturing effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: >85% is world-class

### Availability
- **Formula**: Run Time / Planned Production Time
- **Purpose**: Equipment uptime
- **Benchmarks**: >90% is good

### Performance
- **Formula**: (Ideal Cycle Time × Total Count) / Run Time
- **Purpose**: Speed efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is good

### Quality
- **Formula**: Good Count / Total Count
- **Purpose**: First-pass yield
- **Benchmarks**: >99% is excellent

### Production Rate
- **Formula**: Units Produced / Production Time
- **Purpose**: Output speed
- **Use**: Capacity planning

### Throughput
- **Formula**: Units Produced / Time Period
- **Purpose**: Output measurement
- **Use**: Capacity planning

### Cycle Time
- **Formula**: Total Production Time / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Process speed
- **Use**: Efficiency improvement

### Takt Time
- **Formula**: Available Production Time / Customer Demand
- **Purpose**: Production pacing
- **Use**: Line balancing

## Quality Metrics

### First Pass Yield (FPY)
- **Formula**: Good Units / Total Units Produced × 100%
- **Purpose**: Quality at first pass
- **Benchmarks**: >98% is excellent

### Defect Rate
- **Formula**: Defective Units / Total Units Produced × 100%
- **Purpose**: Quality level
- **Benchmarks**: <1% is good

### Defects per Million Opportunities (DPMO)
- **Formula**: (Defects / (Units × Opportunities)) × 1,000,000
- **Purpose**: Six Sigma quality
- **Benchmarks**: <3.4 DPMO is Six Sigma level

### Scrap Rate
- **Formula**: Scrap Material / Total Material Used × 100%
- **Purpose**: Material waste
- **Benchmarks**: <2% is good

### Rework Rate
- **Formula**: Units Reworked / Total Units Produced × 100%
- **Purpose**: Quality issues
- **Benchmarks**: <3% is good

### Customer Complaints
- **Formula**: Number of customer complaints
- **Purpose**: External quality
- **Use**: Quality improvement

### Cost of Quality
- **Formula**: Prevention + Appraisal + Internal Failure + External Failure Costs
- **Purpose**: Quality costs
- **Benchmarks**: <5% of revenue is good

## Efficiency Metrics

### Labor Utilization
- **Formula**: Direct Labor Time / Total Available Time × 100%
- **Purpose**: Labor efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: >85% is good

### Machine Utilization
- **Formula**: Machine Run Time / Total Available Time × 100%
- **Purpose**: Equipment usage
- **Benchmarks**: >80% is good

### Capacity Utilization
- **Formula**: Actual Output / Maximum Possible Output × 100%
- **Purpose**: Capacity usage
- **Benchmarks**: 70-85% is optimal

### Changeover Time
- **Formula**: Time from last unit of product A to first unit of product B
- **Purpose**: Setup efficiency
- **Use**: SMED improvement

### Downtime
- **Formula**: Total non-productive time
- **Purpose**: Availability tracking
- **Use**: Improvement prioritization

### Overall Labor Effectiveness (OLE)
- **Formula**: Availability × Performance × Quality (for labor)
- **Purpose**: Labor productivity
- **Benchmarks**: >80% is good

## Inventory Metrics

### Inventory Turns
- **Formula**: COGS / Average Inventory
- **Purpose**: Inventory efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Days of Inventory
- **Formula**: (Average Inventory / COGS) × Days
- **Purpose**: Inventory days
- **Benchmarks**: Lower is better

### Raw Material Inventory
- **Formula**: Value of raw materials on hand
- **Purpose**: Material availability
- **Use**: Procurement planning

### Work-in-Progress (WIP)
- **Formula**: Value of partially completed products
- **Purpose**: Process flow
- **Use**: Bottleneck identification

### Finished Goods Inventory
- **Formula**: Value of completed products
- **Purpose**: Stock availability
- **Use**: Fulfillment planning

### Inventory Accuracy
- **Formula**: (Items Counted / Items in System) × 100%
- **Purpose**: Inventory management
- **Benchmarks**: >98% is excellent

## Maintenance Metrics

### Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)
- **Formula**: Total Run Time / Number of Failures
- **Purpose**: Reliability
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)
- **Formula**: Total Repair Time / Number of Repairs
- **Purpose**: Repair efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Lower is better

### Planned vs Unplanned Maintenance
- **Formula**: Planned Maintenance Hours / Total Maintenance Hours × 100%
- **Purpose**: Maintenance planning
- **Benchmarks**: >80% planned is good

### Maintenance Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Maintenance Cost / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Maintenance efficiency
- **Use**: Budget planning

### Equipment Failure Rate
- **Formula**: Number of Failures / Total Operating Time
- **Purpose**: Reliability
- **Use**: Improvement prioritization

## Cost Metrics

### Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Production Cost / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Unit economics
- **Use**: Pricing decisions

### Material Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Material Cost / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Material efficiency
- **Use**: Cost reduction

### Labor Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Labor Cost / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Labor efficiency
- **Use**: Automation decisions

### Overhead Cost per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Overhead / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Overhead allocation
- **Use**: Cost structure

### Total Manufacturing Cost
- **Formula**: Materials + Labor + Overhead
- **Purpose**: Total cost
- **Use**: Profitability analysis

### Cost Variance
- **Formula**: (Actual Cost - Standard Cost) / Standard Cost × 100%
- **Purpose**: Cost control
- **Use**: Variance analysis

## Delivery Metrics

### On-Time Delivery
- **Formula**: Orders Delivered on Time / Total Orders × 100%
- **Purpose**: Delivery performance
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

### Lead Time
- **Formula**: Days from order to delivery
- **Purpose**: Delivery speed
- **Use**: Customer service

### Fill Rate
- **Formula**: Units Shipped / Units Ordered × 100%
- **Purpose**: Order fulfillment
- **Benchmarks**: >98% is excellent

### Perfect Order Rate
- **Formula**: Orders meeting all criteria (on time, complete, damage-free, correct documentation) / Total Orders × 100%
- **Purpose**: Order quality
- **Benchmarks**: >95% is excellent

### Backorder Rate
- **Formula**: Backordered Items / Total Items Ordered × 100%
- **Purpose**: Stock availability
- **Benchmarks**: <2% is good

## Safety Metrics

### Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)
- **Formula**: (Lost Time Injuries × 1,000,000) / Total Hours Worked
- **Purpose**: Safety performance
- **Benchmarks**: <1.0 is excellent

### Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR)
- **Formula**: (Recordable Incidents × 200,000) / Total Hours Worked
- **Purpose**: Safety performance
- **Benchmarks**: <2.0 is good

### Near Miss Reporting Rate
- **Formula**: Near Miss Reports / Total Hours Worked × 1,000,000
- **Purpose**: Safety culture
- **Use**: Prevention strategy

### Safety Training Completion
- **Formula**: Safety Training Completed / Safety Training Assigned × 100%
- **Purpose**: Safety compliance
- **Benchmarks**: 100% is target

### Workers' Compensation Costs
- **Formula**: Total workers' compensation costs
- **Purpose**: Safety cost
- **Use**: ROI of safety programs

- **Purpose**: Waste reduction

### Recycled Material Percentage

- **Purpose**: Sustainability

### Carbon Emissions per Unit
- **Formula**: Total Carbon Emissions / Units Produced
- **Purpose**: Environmental impact
