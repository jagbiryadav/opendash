# HR KPIs

## Recruitment Metrics

### Time to Fill
- **Formula**: Days from job posting to offer acceptance
- **Purpose**: Recruitment efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 30-45 days average, varies by role

### Time to Hire
- **Formula**: Days from candidate application to offer acceptance
- **Purpose**: Process speed
- **Benchmarks**: 20-30 days average

### Cost per Hire
- **Formula**: Total Recruitment Costs / Number of Hires
- **Purpose**: Recruitment efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: $4,000-$5,000 average

### Quality of Hire
- **Formula**: Performance rating × Retention / Time to Productivity
- **Purpose**: Hiring effectiveness
- **Use**: Recruiter performance

### Offer Acceptance Rate
- **Formula**: Offers Accepted / Offers Extended × 100%
- **Purpose**: Employer attractiveness
- **Benchmarks**: >80% is healthy

### Applicant-to-Interview Ratio
- **Formula**: Interviews / Applications
- **Purpose**: Screening efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 10-20% of applicants

### Interview-to-Offer Ratio
- **Formula**: Offers / Interviews
- **Purpose**: Interview effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 20-30% of interviews

## Retention Metrics

### Employee Turnover Rate
- **Formula**: Employees Who Left / Average Number of Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Retention effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: <10% annually is healthy

### Voluntary Turnover Rate
- **Formula**: Voluntary Departures / Average Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Employee satisfaction
- **Benchmarks**: <8% annually is healthy

### Involuntary Turnover Rate
- **Formula**: Involuntary Departures / Average Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Performance management
- **Benchmarks**: <3% annually is healthy

### New Hire Turnover Rate
- **Formula**: New Hires Who Left / Total New Hires × 100%
- **Purpose**: Onboarding effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: <20% in first year

### Retention Rate by Tenure
- **Formula**: Employees Remaining at Tenure / Employees at Start of Tenure × 100%
- **Purpose**: Tenure-based retention
- **Use**: Career path planning

### Retention Rate by Department
- **Formula**: Department Retention / Company Retention
- **Purpose**: Department-specific retention
- **Use**: Management effectiveness

## Engagement Metrics

### Employee Engagement Score
- **Formula**: Average of engagement survey responses
- **Purpose**: Employee satisfaction
- **Benchmarks**: >70% is excellent

### Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS)
- **Formula**: % Promoters - % Detractors
- **Purpose**: Employee loyalty
- **Benchmarks**: >50 is excellent

### Absenteeism Rate
- **Formula**: Days Absent / Total Workdays × 100%
- **Purpose**: Employee health and engagement
- **Benchmarks**: <3% is healthy

### Presenteeism Rate
- **Formula**: Employees Working While Sick / Total Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Employee health
- **Use**: Wellness program design

### Participation Rate
- **Formula**: Employees Participating in Programs / Total Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Program effectiveness
- **Use**: Program optimization

## Performance Metrics

### Goal Completion Rate
- **Formula**: Goals Met / Total Goals × 100%
- **Purpose**: Performance management
- **Benchmarks**: 70-80% is healthy

### Performance Rating Distribution
- **Formula**: Distribution of performance ratings
- **Purpose**: Performance management
- **Use**: Compensation planning

### High Performer Retention Rate
- **Formula**: High Performers Retained / Total High Performers × 100%
- **Purpose**: Top talent retention
- **Benchmarks**: >90% is healthy

### Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) Success Rate
- **Formula**: PIPs Completed Successfully / Total PIPs × 100%
- **Purpose**: PIP effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 50-70% is healthy

### 90-Day Success Rate
- **Formula**: New Hires Meeting Expectations at 90 Days / Total New Hires × 100%
- **Purpose**: Onboarding effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: >80% is healthy

## Compensation Metrics

### Compensation Ratio
- **Formula**: Employee Salary / Market Midpoint
- **Purpose**: Pay equity
- **Benchmarks**: 0.8-1.2 is typical range

### Pay Equity Ratio
- **Formula**: Median Pay for Underrepresented Group / Median Pay for Reference Group
- **Purpose**: Pay equity
- **Benchmarks**: >0.95 is healthy

### Compensation as % of Revenue
- **Formula**: Total Compensation / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Labor cost efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 30-40% for service businesses

### Benefits Cost per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Benefits Cost / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Benefits efficiency
- **Use**: Benefits planning

### Total Compensation Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Total Comp - Previous Total Comp) / Previous Total Comp × 100%
- **Purpose**: Compensation trends
- **Use**: Budget planning

## Training Metrics

### Training Hours per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Training Hours / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Learning investment
- **Benchmarks**: 40-60 hours annually

### Training Cost per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Training Costs / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Learning efficiency
- **Use**: Budget planning

### Training Completion Rate
- **Formula**: Training Completed / Training Assigned × 100%
- **Purpose**: Training engagement
- **Benchmarks**: >80% is healthy

### Training Effectiveness
- **Formula**: Pre-Training Score vs Post-Training Score
- **Purpose**: Learning outcomes
- **Use**: Training program design

### Skills Gap Closure Rate
- **Formula**: Skills Gaps Closed / Total Skills Gaps × 100%
- **Purpose**: Development effectiveness
- **Use**: Workforce planning

## Workforce Metrics

### Headcount
- **Formula**: Total number of employees
- **Purpose**: Workforce size
- **Use**: Planning, benchmarking

### Headcount Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Headcount - Previous Headcount) / Previous Headcount × 100%
- **Purpose**: Growth tracking
- **Use**: Planning

### Revenue per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Revenue / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Productivity
- **Benchmarks**: Varies by industry

### Profit per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Profit / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Productivity
- **Use**: Benchmarking

### Labor Cost per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Labor Costs / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Cost efficiency
- **Use**: Budgeting

### Overtime Hours
- **Formula**: Total overtime hours worked
- **Purpose**: Workload balance
- **Use**: Staffing decisions

## Diversity & Inclusion Metrics

### Diversity Ratio
- **Formula**: Underrepresented Group / Total Employees × 100%
- **Purpose**: Diversity progress
- **Use**: D&I strategy

### Inclusion Index
- **Formula**: Average of inclusion survey responses
- **Purpose**: Inclusion effectiveness
- **Use**: Culture assessment

### Promotion Rate by Demographic
- **Formula**: Promotions for Demographic / Total Promotions × 100%
- **Purpose**: Promotion equity
- **Use**: D&I strategy

### Pay Gap
- **Formula**: (Reference Group Pay - Underrepresented Group Pay) / Reference Group Pay × 100%
- **Purpose**: Pay equity
- **Use**: Compensation strategy

### Supplier Diversity Spend
- **Formula**: Spend with Diverse Suppliers / Total Spend × 100%
- **Purpose**: Supplier diversity
- **Use**: Procurement strategy

## Safety Metrics

### Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)
- **Formula**: (Lost Time Injuries × 1,000,000) / Total Hours Worked
- **Purpose**: Safety performance
- **Benchmarks**: <1.0 is excellent

### Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR)
- **Formula**: (Recordable Incidents × 200,000) / Total Hours Worked
- **Purpose**: Safety performance
- **Benchmarks**: <2.0 is good

### Near Miss Reporting Rate
- **Formula**: Near Miss Reports / Total Hours Worked × 1,000,000
- **Purpose**: Safety culture
- **Use**: Prevention strategy

### Safety Training Completion
- **Formula**: Safety Training Completed / Safety Training Assigned × 100%
- **Purpose**: Safety compliance
- **Benchmarks**: 100% is target

### Workers' Compensation Costs
- **Formula**: Total workers' compensation costs
- **Purpose**: Safety cost
- **Use**: ROI of safety programs
