# Finance KPIs

## Profitability Metrics

### Gross Profit Margin
- **Formula**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Product/service profitability
- **Benchmarks**: 40-60% for software, varies by industry

### Operating Profit Margin
- **Formula**: Operating Income / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Operational efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 15-25% for healthy businesses

### Net Profit Margin
- **Formula**: Net Income / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Overall profitability
- **Benchmarks**: 10-20% for healthy businesses

### EBITDA Margin
- **Formula**: EBITDA / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Cash flow profitability
- **Use**: Valuation, comparison

### Return on Equity (ROE)
- **Formula**: Net Income / Shareholder Equity × 100%
- **Purpose**: Shareholder return
- **Benchmarks**: >15% is excellent

### Return on Assets (ROA)
- **Formula**: Net Income / Total Assets × 100%
- **Purpose**: Asset utilization
- **Benchmarks**: >5% is good

### Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)
- **Formula**: NOPAT / Invested Capital × 100%
- **Purpose**: Capital efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: >WACC creates value

## Liquidity Metrics

### Current Ratio
- **Formula**: Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Short-term solvency
- **Benchmarks**: 1.5-2.0 is healthy

### Quick Ratio
- **Formula**: (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Immediate solvency
- **Benchmarks**: >1.0 is healthy

### Cash Ratio
- **Formula**: Cash / Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Most conservative liquidity measure
- **Benchmarks**: >0.5 is healthy

### Working Capital
- **Formula**: Current Assets - Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Operational liquidity
- **Use**: Growth funding

### Cash Conversion Cycle
- **Formula**: DSO + DIO - DPO
- **Purpose**: Cash efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Lower is better

## Efficiency Metrics

### Accounts Receivable Turnover
- **Formula**: Revenue / Average Accounts Receivable
- **Purpose**: Collection efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
- **Formula**: (Accounts Receivable / Revenue) × Days
- **Purpose**: Collection speed
- **Benchmarks**: <30 days is excellent

### Inventory Turnover
- **Formula**: COGS / Average Inventory
- **Purpose**: Inventory efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)
- **Formula**: (Inventory / COGS) × Days
- **Purpose**: Inventory speed
- **Benchmarks**: Lower is better

### Accounts Payable Turnover
- **Formula**: COGS / Average Accounts Payable
- **Purpose**: Payment efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Industry-specific

### Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)
- **Formula**: (Accounts Payable / COGS) × Days
- **Purpose**: Payment speed
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better (within terms)

## Leverage Metrics

### Debt-to-Equity Ratio
- **Formula**: Total Debt / Total Equity
- **Purpose**: Financial leverage
- **Benchmarks**: <1.0 is conservative, varies by industry

### Debt-to-Assets Ratio
- **Formula**: Total Debt / Total Assets
- **Purpose**: Asset financing
- **Benchmarks**: <0.5 is healthy

### Interest Coverage Ratio
- **Formula**: EBIT / Interest Expense
- **Purpose**: Debt service ability
- **Benchmarks**: >3.0 is healthy

### Leverage Ratio
- **Formula**: Total Assets / Total Equity
- **Purpose**: Financial leverage
- **Benchmarks**: <2.0 is conservative

## Cash Flow Metrics

### Operating Cash Flow
- **Formula**: Cash from operations
- **Purpose**: Core business cash generation
- **Use**: Sustainability assessment

### Free Cash Flow
- **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures
- **Purpose**: Cash available for distribution
- **Use**: Valuation, dividend capacity

### Cash Flow Margin
- **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Cash generation efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: >10% is healthy

### Cash Flow Return on Investment
- **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow / Invested Capital
- **Purpose**: Cash efficiency
- **Use**: Valuation

## Valuation Metrics

### Price-to-Earnings (P/E)
- **Formula**: Stock Price / Earnings per Share
- **Purpose**: Market valuation
- **Benchmarks**: 15-25 is average, varies by growth

### Price-to-Sales (P/S)
- **Formula**: Market Cap / Revenue
- **Purpose**: Revenue-based valuation
- **Use**: High-growth companies

### Price-to-Book (P/B)
- **Formula**: Market Cap / Book Value
- **Purpose**: Asset-based valuation
- **Benchmarks**: <1.0 may be undervalued

### Enterprise Value-to-EBITDA (EV/EBITDA)
- **Formula**: Enterprise Value / EBITDA
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive valuation
- **Benchmarks**: 10-15 is average

### Price-to-Cash Flow
- **Formula**: Stock Price / Cash Flow per Share
- **Purpose**: Cash flow valuation
- **Use**: Capital-intensive industries

## Growth Metrics

### Revenue Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Revenue - Previous Revenue) / Previous Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Top-line growth
- **Use**: Valuation, strategy

### Earnings Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Earnings - Previous Earnings) / Previous Earnings × 100%
- **Purpose**: Profitability growth
- **Use**: Valuation

### Book Value Growth
- **Formula**: (Current Book Value - Previous Book Value) / Previous Book Value × 100%
- **Purpose**: Equity growth
- **Use**: Long-term value creation

### Dividend Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Dividend - Previous Dividend) / Previous Dividend × 100%
- **Purpose**: Dividend sustainability
- **Use**: Income investing

## Working Capital Metrics

### Net Working Capital
- **Formula**: Current Assets - Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Operational liquidity
- **Use**: Growth funding

### Working Capital Ratio
- **Formula**: Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Liquidity assessment
- **Benchmarks**: 1.5-2.0 is healthy

### Working Capital Turnover
- **Formula**: Revenue / Working Capital
- **Purpose**: Working capital efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Higher is better

### Cash Conversion Efficiency
- **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow / Net Income
- **Purpose**: Earnings quality
- **Benchmarks**: >1.0 is healthy

## Budgeting Metrics

### Budget Variance
- **Formula**: (Actual - Budget) / Budget × 100%
- **Purpose**: Budget accuracy
- **Use**: Planning improvement

### Forecast Accuracy
- **Formula**: 1 - |Actual - Forecast| / Actual
- **Purpose**: Forecast quality
- **Benchmarks**: >90% is excellent

### Capital Expenditure Ratio
- **Formula**: CapEx / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Investment intensity
- **Use**: Growth planning

### Research & Development Ratio
- **Formula**: R&D Spend / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Innovation investment
- **Benchmarks**: 10-20% for tech

## Risk Metrics

### Z-Score (Altman)
- **Formula**: 1.2×Working Capital/Assets + 1.4×Retained Earnings/Assets + 3.3×EBIT/Assets + 0.6×Market Cap/Total Liabilities + 1.0×Revenue/Assets
- **Purpose**: Bankruptcy prediction
- **Benchmarks**: >2.99 is safe, <1.81 is distress

### Interest Coverage
- **Formula**: EBIT / Interest Expense
- **Purpose**: Debt service ability
- **Benchmarks**: >3.0 is healthy

### Debt Service Coverage Ratio
- **Formula**: Operating Income / Total Debt Service
- **Purpose**: Debt repayment ability
- **Benchmarks**: >1.25 is healthy

### Current Asset Coverage
- **Formula**: Current Assets / Total Liabilities
- **Purpose**: Short-term coverage
- **Benchmarks**: >1.0 is healthy
