# Sales KPIs

## Revenue Metrics

### Total Revenue
- **Formula**: Σ(Price × Quantity)
- **Purpose**: Overall sales performance
- **Granularity**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly

### Revenue Growth Rate
- **Formula**: (Current Period Revenue - Previous Period Revenue) / Previous Period Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Trend analysis, growth tracking
- **Benchmarks**: Industry-specific, typically 10-20% YoY for healthy businesses

### Revenue per Customer
- **Formula**: Total Revenue / Number of Customers
- **Purpose**: Customer value assessment
- **Use**: Segmentation, targeting

### Revenue per Employee
- **Formula**: Total Revenue / Number of Employees
- **Purpose**: Operational efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: Varies by industry

## Volume Metrics

### Units Sold
- **Formula**: Σ(Quantity)
- **Purpose**: Volume tracking
- **Use**: Inventory planning, demand forecasting

### Sales Volume Growth
- **Formula**: (Current Period Units - Previous Period Units) / Previous Period Units × 100%
- **Purpose**: Volume trend analysis

### Average Order Size
- **Formula**: Total Units / Number of Orders
- **Purpose**: Basket size optimization

## Customer Metrics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Formula**: Total Sales & Marketing Costs / Number of New Customers
- **Purpose**: Efficiency of customer acquisition
- **Benchmarks**: Should be < LTV/3

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Formula**: Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
- **Purpose**: Long-term customer value
- **Use**: Budget allocation, segmentation

### LTV/CAC Ratio
- **Formula**: LTV / CAC
- **Purpose**: Sustainability of growth
- **Benchmarks**: >3.0 is healthy, <1.0 is unsustainable

### Churn Rate
- **Formula**: Customers Lost / Total Customers at Start × 100%
- **Purpose**: Customer retention
- **Benchmarks**: <5% monthly for SaaS, varies by industry

### Retention Rate
- **Formula**: (Customers at End - New Customers) / Customers at Start × 100%
- **Purpose**: Customer loyalty
- **Use**: Valuation, growth prediction

## Efficiency Metrics

### Sales Conversion Rate
- **Formula**: Number of Sales / Number of Leads × 100%
- **Purpose**: Sales funnel efficiency
- **Benchmarks**: 2-5% for B2B, varies by industry

### Sales Cycle Length
- **Formula**: Average days from lead to close
- **Purpose**: Sales process efficiency
- **Use**: Forecasting, resource planning

### Win Rate
- **Formula**: Number of Deals Won / Total Deals × 100%
- **Purpose**: Competitive effectiveness
- **Benchmarks**: 20-30% for B2B

### Quote-to-Close Ratio
- **Formula**: Number of Closed Deals / Number of Quotes Sent × 100%
- **Purpose**: Proposal effectiveness

### Revenue per Sales Rep
- **Formula**: Total Revenue / Number of Sales Reps
- **Purpose**: Sales team productivity

## Profitability Metrics

### Gross Margin
- **Formula**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Product profitability
- **Benchmarks**: 40-60% for software, varies by industry

### Contribution Margin
- **Formula**: (Revenue - Variable Costs) / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Product contribution to fixed costs
- **Use**: Pricing decisions, product mix

### Net Profit Margin
- **Formula**: Net Income / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Overall profitability
- **Benchmarks**: 10-20% for healthy businesses

### Return on Sales (ROS)
- **Formula**: Operating Profit / Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Operational efficiency

## Activity Metrics

### Number of Deals
- **Formula**: Count of closed deals
- **Purpose**: Sales activity tracking

### Pipeline Value
- **Formula**: Σ(Deal Value × Probability)
- **Purpose**: Revenue forecasting
- **Use**: Resource planning

### Pipeline Coverage
- **Formula**: Pipeline Value / Revenue Target
- **Purpose**: Target achievability
- **Benchmarks**: 3-4x target

### Activities per Rep
- **Formula**: Total activities / Number of reps
- **Purpose**: Activity benchmarking

### Meetings per Rep
- **Formula**: Total meetings / Number of reps
- **Purpose**: Engagement tracking

## Segmentation Metrics

### Revenue by Product
- **Formula**: Revenue per product/SKU
- **Purpose**: Product performance
- **Use**: Portfolio management

### Revenue by Region
- **Formula**: Revenue per geographic region
- **Purpose**: Geographic performance
- **Use**: Expansion planning

### Revenue by Channel
- **Formula**: Revenue per sales channel
- **Purpose**: Channel effectiveness
- **Use**: Channel strategy

### Revenue by Customer Segment
- **Formula**: Revenue per customer segment
- **Purpose**: Segment profitability
- **Use**: Targeting, pricing

### Revenue by Sales Rep
- **Formula**: Revenue per sales representative
- **Purpose**: Individual performance
- **Use**: Compensation, training

## Trend Metrics

### Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
- **Formula**: (This Month - Last Month) / Last Month × 100%
- **Purpose**: Short-term trend
- **Use**: Operational decisions

### Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) Growth
- **Formula**: (This Quarter - Last Quarter) / Last Quarter × 100%
- **Purpose**: Medium-term trend
- **Use**: Quarterly planning

### Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
- **Formula**: (This Year - Last Year) / Last Year × 100%
- **Purpose**: Long-term trend, seasonality adjustment
- **Use**: Strategic planning

### Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)
- **Formula**: (Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1/Number of Years) - 1
- **Purpose**: Smoothed annual growth rate
- **Use**: Valuation, benchmarking

## Forecasting Metrics

### Forecast Accuracy
- **Formula**: 1 - |Actual - Forecast| / Actual
- **Purpose**: Forecast quality
- **Benchmarks**: >90% is excellent

### Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE)
- **Formula**: Σ|Actual - Forecast| / Actual / n × 100%
- **Purpose**: Forecast error measurement
- **Use**: Model selection

### Bias
- **Formula**: Σ(Forecast - Actual) / n
- **Purpose**: Systematic over/under forecasting
- **Target**: Close to zero

## Benchmarking Metrics

### Market Share
- **Formula**: Company Revenue / Total Market Revenue × 100%
- **Purpose**: Competitive position
- **Use**: Strategy, valuation

### Share of Voice
- **Formula**: Company Mentions / Total Industry Mentions × 100%
- **Purpose**: Brand visibility
- **Use**: Marketing effectiveness

### Competitive Win Rate
- **Formula**: Wins vs Specific Competitor / Total Deals vs That Competitor × 100%
- **Purpose**: Competitive effectiveness
- **Use**: Competitive strategy
