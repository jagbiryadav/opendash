# Marketing Domain Pack

## Overview
Specialized knowledge, metrics, and workflows for marketing analytics.

## Key Metrics

### Acquisition Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Customer Acquisition Cost | Spend / New Customers | <$100 | Acquisition efficiency |
| Cost per Lead | Spend / Leads | <$50 | Lead generation |
| Cost per Click | Spend / Clicks | <$2 | Ad efficiency |
| Conversion Rate | Conversions / Visitors | 2-5% | Funnel effectiveness |
| Bounce Rate | Single-page Sessions / Total | <40% | Engagement |

### Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Click-Through Rate | Clicks / Impressions | 1-3% | Ad effectiveness |
| Time on Page | Total Time / Visits | >2 min | Content quality |
| Pages per Session | Pageviews / Sessions | >3 | Engagement depth |
| Email Open Rate | Opens / Delivered | 20-25% | Subject effectiveness |
| Social Engagement Rate | Interactions / Followers | 1-3% | Content resonance |

### Retention Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Customer Retention Rate | (End - New) / Start × 100 | >80% | Loyalty |
| Churn Rate | Lost / Start × 100 | <5% | Attrition |
| Repeat Purchase Rate | Repeat Buyers / Total | >30% | Loyalty |
| Customer Lifetime Value | AOV × Frequency × Lifespan | >$500 | Long-term value |
| Net Promoter Score | % Promoters - % Detractors | >50 | Advocacy |

### Revenue Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Marketing ROI | (Revenue - Cost) / Cost | >300% | Profitability |
| Return on Ad Spend | Revenue / Ad Spend | >4x | Ad profitability |
| Revenue per Visitor | Revenue / Visitors | >$5 | Monetization |
| Average Order Value | Revenue / Orders | >$100 | Basket size |
| Customer Payback Period | CAC / Monthly Revenue | <12 months | CAC recovery |

### Attribution Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| First-Touch Attribution | Credit to first interaction | Varies | Awareness |
| Last-Touch Attribution | Credit to last interaction | Varies | Conversion |
| Linear Attribution | Equal credit to all touches | Varies | Fairness |
| Time-Decay Attribution | More credit to recent | Varies | Recency |
| Data-Driven Attribution | Algorithm-based credit | Varies | Accuracy |
